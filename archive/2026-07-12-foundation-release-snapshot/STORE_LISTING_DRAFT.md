## Proposed listing

- Name: Pocket Potion Works
- Subtitle: A tiny cozy alchemy workshop
- Category: Games — casual / simulation (**owner to confirm per target store**)

Grow a pocket-sized potion shop at your own pace. Gather whimsical ingredients, brew a shelf of curious elixirs, help villagers with their orders, and improve a warm little workshop one upgrade at a time. Progress continues gently while you are away, capped at four hours.

Features:

- Gather, brew, collect, deliver, and upgrade in short mobile-friendly sessions.
- Eight unlockable recipes, six ingredients, twelve customer variants, daily goals, and achievements.
- Local, versioned progress with capped offline gathering.
- Reduced-motion support, keyboard-accessible essential controls, mobile safe areas, and optional sound that starts off.
- A dependency-free offline web shell with no remote fonts or runtime assets.

Current prototype monetization wording: **No real-money purchases, advertisements, accounts, analytics transmission, or cloud saves are connected.** The Moonlight Market contains clearly labeled simulations only and must not be described as store billing or rewarded advertising.
